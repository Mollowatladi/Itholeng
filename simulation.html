Simulation

Click here to access an online simulation of Natural Selection (This may be done before or after lesson 1)

This simulation explores the survival of prey species individuals based on their coloring and the environment. The user controls the predator in the simulation and "eats" the prey by clicking on them.

HOW TO OPERATE THE SIMULATION:

  1. Choose between grassland, mountain land and desert as your environment.
  2. Click on “run simulation” to get started.
  3. Grey and brown peas will appear on the environment chosen.
  4. You will act as the predator by clicking on the peas randomly.
  5. Click on as many peas as you can until simulation is over.
  6. Results will be shown on the screen once simulation is done.
  7. Start over by picking a different environment and repeat the process of picking peas.
